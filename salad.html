Italian Salad

A quick and easy rustic Italian salad, with a zesty vinagrette. Pefect for a dinner party!

Ingredients

  • Green veggies
  • Tomatoes
  • Croutons
  • Parmesean
  • Nuts
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Dijon
  • Crushed garlic
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Make dressing. Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon, crushed garlic, oregano and salt and pepper together in a bowl until combinded.
  2. Mix the salad. Combine green veggies, tomatoes, croutons, parmesean and nuts together in a large bowl.
  3. Dress the salad. Drizzle your dressing over your salad evenly and toss to combine.
  4. Serve. Serve immediately while it's fresh!
