Tiramisu

A delicious and ridiculously easy no bake recipie for an Italian favourite.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Heavy whipped cream
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Mix filling. Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together to form stiff peaks.
  2. Dip ladyfingers. Add coffee to a bowl and dip the ladyfingers on both sides quickly.
  3. Layer mascarpone. Smooth a layer of the mascarpone mixture on top of the lady fingers.
  4. Repeat. Add another layer of ladyfingers and another layer of mascarpone mixture.
  5. Finish and serve. Dust with the cocoa powder and serve.
