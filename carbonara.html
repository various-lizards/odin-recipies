Carbonara

A traditional Italian carbonara. Rich and creamy, impress your dinner guests with this simple yet tasty recipie.

Ingredients

  • Guanciale. Alternatively use bacon or pancetta if you can't get guanciale.
  • 2 egg yolks
  • Parmigiano reggiano cheese
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Slice. Cut your guanciale into thick batons.
  2. Grate. Finely grate your cheese and set to one side.
  3. Whisk. Whisk together your egg yolks with the cheese and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Cook pasta. Boil 4 litres of water with 1 tablespoon of salt. Cook your pasta per packet directions and reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water in a cup before draining.
  5. Cook guanciale. While the pasta is cooking, cooked your guanciale until golden brown.
  6. Toss pasta in guanciale. Add your hot pasta to the pan with guanciale and toss so the pasta is coated.
  7. Add egg and pasta water. Remove the pan from the heat and add your egg and pasta water.
  8. Mix. Combine the all the ingredients together and watch the sauce turn creamy.
  9. Serve. Serve immediately while still warm from the pan and enjoy!
